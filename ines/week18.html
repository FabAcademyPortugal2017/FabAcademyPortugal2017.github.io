Intellectual property

This project is freely opened to everyone who is interested.
In brief this project can be, and needs to be, developed in different directions.
This is why I think the license shouldn't limit ate all the developing chance.
As consequence I think it's correct to share this project through on of
the Creative Commons License formulas. More in details I want to let others remix,
tweak, build upon my work even for commercial purposes, as long as they credit me and license
their new creations under the identical terms.All new works based on yours will carry the same
license, so any derivatives will also allow commercial use. This corresponds to the:
Attribution-Share Alike [CC BY-SA] license
