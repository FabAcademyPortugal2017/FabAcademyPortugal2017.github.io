Hello,
I'm André Rocha

Product and Interaction Designer & wannabe farmer

I'm a senior Product and Interaction designer (Founder at EVOL/LEVO) and professor (ESELx - IPL // ESAD.cr) currently enrolled in the UT Austin | Portugal Digital Media PhD program.

I started my career in 2003 designing products along with a group of Artisans in southern Portugal (Alentejo).

Somehow, in the middle of advertising agency work, trade fair stands, big events, design and production discovered arduino, processing, code, and a lot of things I came to admire, explore, use and research with and about.

My previous experience led me to this balance between two parallel environments where I design, teach and research: the tech/hacking/ DiY/collaborative/open source and the craft product design experiences.

I was part of the team who founded DAR, back in 2011: An Open Design and Culture Non-profit, where I manage the instalation of Fab Lab Caldas da Rainha.

I also collaborate with Creative Commons as Portugal team Technical Lead.
