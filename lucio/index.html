      About

      Lucio Pentagna is passionate about tech hardware, 3D printers, digital manufacturing, and prototyping. A former executive, he transformed his life, sailed the Atlantic, and founded the first Fab Lab in China, XinFab. He is now building Algarve Fab Farm in Portugal.

      My lab - Algarve Fab Farm

      Discover Algarve Fab Farm, a unique Fab Lab nestled in the scenic western Algarve, Portugal. Located on peaceful farmland, yet just 20 minutes from stunning surf beaches, it offers a tranquil and inspiring environment. Beyond standard Fab Lab equipment, we provide accommodation for guests, sponsored residents, and staff, fostering a collaborative community.

      Algarve Fab Farm is dedicated to supporting:

      • Local farmers innovating with new agricultural techniques and tools.
      • Startups and companies seeking a focused retreat to advance their projects.
      • Residents developing impactful projects related to sustainable farming and technology.
      • Makers and hackers looking for a creative escape and project development space.

      Final Project: Smart Irrigation Controller

      Automate your garden with the Smart Irrigation Controller, my Fab Academy final project. This system intelligently schedules irrigation based on zones and time, optimizing water usage and plant health.

      Key features include remote scheduling, real-time humidity and temperature monitoring, and a PCB designed for future expansion. Explore how this project can revolutionize your irrigation practices, saving water and time.
