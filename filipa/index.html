About Me

My name is Ana Filipa Silva, I'm 23 years old and I'm from Setubal, a city in Portugal.

I am a curious girl and since I was little I started to playing and disassemble objects to try to find out how things are done, which later led me to study design. The things that make me happy are traveling, eating, meet new people and get into small adventures.

I know how to model and I had the opportunity to print some of my projects in 3D, however I would like to expand my knowledge and learn more about digital fabrication, electronic and programming.
