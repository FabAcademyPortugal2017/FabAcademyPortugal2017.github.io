Computer-aided design

Week 2

3D modeling

To make the 3D model of my object, I used the software Rhinoceros3D that works with surfaces, and allows to draw my product in detail and allows to apply the right measures to then have the technical drawing of my object.

Render and Animation

After having modeled my object, I used the Keyshot program to give some textures to the different parts of my object. To begin with, I've applied simple textures, like wood and plastics, because I will certainly have to make more changes over time to my 3D model.

Then with the help of AdobePremier program I added some subtitles to the video to identify each part of my project.
